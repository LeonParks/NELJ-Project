NELJ Group Project

The aim of our project is to present the data from our chosen dataset using appropriate statistical techniques. We hope that our methods to visualise the data can aid businesses in making intelligent decisions and solve problems. The dataset contains the different costs of cancer treatment in the US.

The NELJ Business Systems Development Team consists of Teesside Students: Niall, Elliot, Leon, and Josh.
